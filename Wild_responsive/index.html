ABOUT THE WILD LION

Deep within a forest, exists an extraordinary world, a world of lions. They work, play, fight and look for love in a non-stop riot of energy in motion. And now our company brought them to you to show their world, their bravery and their capacity of entertainment. They are the masters of balls and jumping!

ABOUT THE WILD TIGER

This fantastic adventure takes you to the land of tigers, where King Amarice leads the community. These wild tigers can do anything for their King. This time Amarice will show you his best tigers who have no fear of fire at all.

ABOUT THE WILD ELEPHANT

And to finish our spectacular show we will lead you to the world of Wild Elephants. Follow the adventure of a young elephant and his friends who can dance, sing and play for you in a way you've never seen before.
